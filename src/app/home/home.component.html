ABOUT TALLGRASS

TALLGRASS - PASSIONATE AND DEDICATED IT SKILL DEVELOPMENT CENTER

TallGrass was founded with a vision to bridge the employability gap between unemployed youth and the industry. We offer comprehensive learning solutions to individuals, colleges and corporate. Leveraging our strength we manage programs focused on Skill Development and Employability through innovation and technology. We take pride in our unique methods of skill development by leveraging the use of technology, classroom delivery and our content to enhance both the learning and skill development experience. We believe in cultivating young minds to make them smart thinkers and industry ready. Our cutting-edge solutions for students and professionals help them to carve a niche for themselves in the industry. The focus of the company is to establish, manage and operate skill development centers across India to act as a bridge between unemployed youth and the industries seeking skilled manpower.
